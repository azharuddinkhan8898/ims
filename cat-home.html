EVERY CAT IS UNIQUE

Did you know

Indoor:

Indoor cats require a diet that’s high in protein & low in calories
The right diet can reduce hairballs before they start
Indoor cats are more prone to weight related problems
Indoor cats spend 70% of their day sleeping

Senior:

Cats show age- related changes after 7 years
Senior cats are susceptible to dental issues
The immunity of cats tends to fall as they mature
Abrupt changes in routine affect senior cats

Adults:

Cats are actually carnivores
Cats calorie intake depends on their level of activity
Cats are susceptible to obesity & nutrition-related problems
Cats require taurine, for normal heart function

Kitten:

Kittens require a higher nutrient requirement
Kittens grow quickly, maturing in 10-12 months
Kittens are susceptible to nutritional imbalance
Kittens need more digestible food than adults

So why feed them
the same generic food?
